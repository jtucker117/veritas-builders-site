What's Included

Earthwork the next trade can build on.

Bad dirt work compounds — every trade above it pays for it. We grade and compact the way it needs to be done so the concrete contractor isn't fixing slope and the building isn't settling two years in.

01

Rough & Finish Grading

Strip topsoil, balance cut and fill, shape to design elevations, finish-grade to plan tolerance — verified with rover or laser before pad turnover.

02

Pad Sites & Building Pads

Building pad construction with select fill, lift-by-lift compaction, density testing, and a compaction report the civil engineer signs off on.

03

Drainage & Detention

Stormwater swales, detention pond cuts, storm pipe and inlets, outfall structures — built to the civil drawings and as-built for the inspector.

04

Base Prep & Erosion Control

Subgrade prep for concrete or asphalt paving, lime or cement stabilization where the geotech calls for it, plus TCEQ-compliant SWPPP install and maintenance.

How It Works

From plan-set to build-ready dirt.

1

Plan & Geotech Review

We review the civil plans, geotech report, and survey, walk the site, and flag anything that affects the bid — rock, wet ground, fill availability, access.

2

Earthwork Bid

Written line-item bid: clearing, grubbing, strip, cut/fill quantities, import or export, pad build, drainage, base prep, and SWPPP. No mystery line items.

3

Build to Grade

SWPPP installed first, then earthwork in the sequence the schedule calls for. Compaction tested per spec. Daily progress photos so the GC and engineer know exactly where we are.

4

Turnover & As-Builts

Final grade verified, density reports delivered, drainage as-builts marked up, erosion controls maintained until vertical takes over. Clean handoff to the next trade.
